INDIAN DOCTORS NETWORK(FOR DOCTORS)

Network of doctors around India/

Indian Doctors Network is an exclusive app for all doctors in India. It provides a safe platform for medical professionals to discuss cases and queries, participate in quizzes and polls, and share knowledge in a spam-free environment.
Indian Doctors Network is designed to integrate technology seamlessly into the lives of medical care providers to make patient care more efficient, by helping doctors stay current and connected with the entire medical fraternity effortlessly. This app is packed with useful features, has a zero learning curve and is extremely easy to use. Key features:
CMEs and medical events:
Now you don’t have to miss out on fantastic educational opportunities to enhance your skills and knowledge. As a doctor, it is important to find out the most relevant and upcoming Continuing Medical Education, related events and workshops taking place around you, and that’s why we bring to you our unique CME feature. Book CMEs right through the app.
Medical cases:
You can create case studies easily to discuss and get help with patient diagnosis, prognosis and treatment planning, and all things related to patient care. You can also participate in multiple cases created by other doctors share knowledge.
Medical quizzes:
You are a great doctor, and you want to take a break from your swamped schedule to unwind, without compromising on your medical care acumen. Play exciting quizzes created by doctors, for doctors, about medicine on Indian Doctors Network, along with your friends connected within the app! Or even better, create a quiz and challenge everyone.
Medical jobs:
You have access to a whole portal of medical jobs at your disposal. Post or find relevant medical jobs, as per your interest and preferred metrics, and our smart system keeps learning and improving, based on your usage and feedback.
Medical communities:
You can join multiple communities, and interact with fellow medical professionals on Indian Doctors Network, including your specialty- based groups, alumnus, city groups, medical association and many more. Be a part of group activities and benefit from the community.
Medical connections:
This is an all-encompassing platform for medical professionals and not just an app for doctors. Connect with any doctor you know or any doctor you would like to know and grow your professional network.
Post:
Have a question to ask or an anecdote to share with your connections? Create a post and share it with your network. Have organized discussions on IDN, regarding new techniques, innovations, research or practice, and finance management.
Medical associations:
Is your association on-boarded with Indian Doctors Network yet? If yes, find it and join it on IDN, and enjoy all the amazing features within your association, besides staying in sync with your association’s events and updates (announcement, circulars, notices, and news). If no, well, what are you waiting for, let us bring your association online?
Bookmark:
Start bookmarking all the things you find interesting and would like to get back to sometime later, effortlessly. No more finding the needle in a haystack. Also get instant notifications to never miss any update, ever.
With the myriad of existing features and many more exciting features coming soon, you will find IDN to be not just your favorite app, but your go-to instrument for everything related to medicine.
Be a part of an intellectually enriching community experience. Be a part of the Indian Doctors Network!
