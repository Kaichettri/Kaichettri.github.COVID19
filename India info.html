COVID in India

India is the seventh largest country in the world and the second most populous country.

India which was once considered as "A Heaven in the world" by many of the greatest kings like Alexander The Great . Presently , India was affected by COVID-19.Sadly at present ,India has the third most number of COVID-19 cases in the world beneath USA and Brazil.


Now the Indian government is taking many steps in reducing COVID-19.Infact India did well in the first half period of COVID-19 outcome when all the developed countries are brutally attacked by the COVID-19 wave.


Small history of COVID-19 in India

The first COVID-19 cases in India was recorded in Kerala in the month of January 2020.

In March second half ,the Indian government declared a national wide lockdown for a few days.Even after that the condition is not improved still.Thus the government still extended the lockdown untill May 31.
